Introduction

pH levels in the soil or growing medium significantly impact plant health. Maintaining the correct pH range is crucial for the absorption of nutrients and overall plant growth and vitality.

pH Effects

Soil that is too acidic (low pH) or alkaline (high pH) can lead to nutrient deficiencies, poor root development, and reduced plant growth. Different plants have different pH preferences.

Optimal pH Range

Research the specific pH requirements of the plants you are cultivating. Soil pH can be adjusted using various amendments to create the ideal growing conditions for your plants.
