Introduction

Temperature is a critical factor affecting plant health. Extreme temperatures, whether too hot or too cold, can have adverse effects on plants. Maintaining an optimal temperature range is essential for their growth and well-being.

Temperature Effects

High temperatures can lead to heat stress, causing wilting, scorching, and reduced photosynthesis. Conversely, low temperatures can result in frost damage, slowed growth, and even plant death.

Optimal Temperature Range

Each plant species has its preferred temperature range for optimal growth. It's crucial to research and understand the specific temperature requirements of the plants you are cultivating to ensure their health and productivity.
